三、正向扩散过程

1、给定初始数据分布 x0∼q(x)x_0 \sim q(x)x0​∼q(x) ，可以不断地向分布中添加高斯噪声， 该噪声的标准差是以固定值 βt\beta_tβt​ 而确定的，均值是以固定值 βt\beta_tβt​ 和当前t时亥 的数据 xtx_txt​ 决定的。这个过程是一个马尔科夫链过程.
2、随着t的不断增大，最终数据分布 xTx_TxT​ 变成了一个各向独立的高斯分布。

q(xt∣xt−1)=N(xt;1−βtxt−1,βtI)q(x1:T∣x0)=∏t=1Tq(xt∣xt−1)q\left(\mathbf{x}_t \mid \mathbf{x}_{t-1}\right)=\mathcal{N}\left(\mathbf{x}_t ; \sqrt{1-\beta_t} \mathbf{x}_{t-1}, \beta_t \mathbf{I}\right) \quad q\left(\mathbf{x}_{1: T} \mid \mathbf{x}_0\right)=\prod_{t=1}^T q\left(\mathbf{x}_t \mid \mathbf{x}_{t-1}\right) q(xt​∣xt−1​)=N(xt​;1−βt​​xt−1​,βt​I)q(x1:T​∣x0​)=t=1∏T​q(xt​∣xt−1​)

用重参数化技巧可以等价与下面

xt=αtxt−1+1−αtzt−1; where zt−1,zt−2,⋯∼N(0,I)\mathbf{x}_t=\sqrt{\alpha_t} \mathbf{x}_{t-1}+\sqrt{1-\alpha_t} \mathbf{z}_{t-1} \quad ; \text { where } \mathbf{z}_{t-1}, \mathbf{z}_{t-2}, \cdots \sim \mathcal{N}(\mathbf{0}, \mathbf{I}) xt​=αt​​xt−1​+1−αt​​zt−1​; where zt−1​,zt−2​,⋯∼N(0,I)

一直递推下去便可以得到

xt=αtxt−1+1−αtzt−1; where zt−1,zt−2,⋯∼N(0,I)=αtαt−1xt−2+1−αtαt−1z‾t−2; where z‾t−2 merges two Gaussians (∗).=…=αˉtx0+1−αˉtzq(xt∣x0)=N(xt;αˉtx0,(1−αˉt)I)\begin{array}{rlr} \mathbf{x}_t & =\sqrt{\alpha_t} \mathbf{x}_{t-1}+\sqrt{1-\alpha_t} \mathbf{z}_{t-1} \quad ;\text { where } \mathbf{z}_{t-1}, \mathbf{z}_{t-2}, \cdots \sim \mathcal{N}(\mathbf{0}, \mathbf{I}) \\ & =\sqrt{\alpha_t \alpha_{t-1}} \mathbf{x}_{t-2}+\sqrt{1-\alpha_t \alpha_{t-1}} \overline{\mathbf{z}}_{t-2} \quad ; \text { where } \overline{\mathbf{z}}_{t-2} \text { merges two Gaussians }(*) . \\ & =\ldots \\ & =\sqrt{\bar{\alpha}_t} \mathbf{x}_0+\sqrt{1-\bar{\alpha}_t} \mathbf{z} \\ q\left(\mathbf{x}_t \mid \mathbf{x}_0\right) & =\mathcal{N}\left(\mathbf{x}_t ; \sqrt{\bar{\alpha}_t} \mathbf{x}_0,\left(1-\bar{\alpha}_t\right) \mathbf{I}\right) \end{array} xt​q(xt​∣x0​)​=αt​​xt−1​+1−αt​​zt−1​; where zt−1​,zt−2​,⋯∼N(0,I)=αt​αt−1​​xt−2​+1−αt​αt−1​​zt−2​; where zt−2​ merges two Gaussians (∗).=…=αˉt​​x0​+1−αˉt​​z=N(xt​;αˉt​​x0​,(1−αˉt​)I)​

即 xt\mathbf{x}_txt​ 可以直接由 x0\mathbf{x}_0x0​ 推得。

四、逆扩散过程

目标是

pθ(xt−1∣xt)=N(xt−1;μθ(xt,t),Σθ(xt,t))p_\theta\left(\mathbf{x}_{t-1} \mid \mathbf{x}_t\right)=\mathcal{N}\left(\mathbf{x}_{t-1} ; \boldsymbol{\mu}_\theta\left(\mathbf{x}_t, t\right), \mathbf{\Sigma}_\theta\left(\mathbf{x}_t, t\right)\right)pθ​(xt−1​∣xt​)=N(xt−1​;μθ​(xt​,t),Σθ​(xt​,t))

，求得其中

μθ(xt,t)\boldsymbol{\mu}_\theta\left(\mathbf{x}_t, t\right)μθ​(xt​,t) 和 Σθ(xt,t)\mathbf{\Sigma}_\theta\left(\mathbf{x}_t, t\right)Σθ​(xt​,t)

即可

根据马尔克夫假设

pθ(xt−1∣xt)=q(xt−1∣xt,x0)=q(xt∣xt−1,x0)q(xt−1∣x0)q(xt∣x0)∝exp⁡(−12((xt−αtxt−1)2βt+(xt−1−αt−1x0)21−αˉt−1−(xt−αˉtx0)21−αˉt))=exp⁡(−12((αtβt+11−αˉt−1)xt−12−(2αtβtxt+2αˉt1−αˉtx0)xt−1+C(xt,x0)))\begin{aligned} p_\theta\left(\mathbf{x}_{t-1} \mid \mathbf{x}_t\right)=q\left(\mathbf{x}_{t-1} \mid \mathbf{x}_t, \mathbf{x}_0\right) & =q\left(\mathbf{x}_t \mid \mathbf{x}_{t-1}, \mathbf{x}_0\right) \frac{q\left(\mathbf{x}_{t-1} \mid \mathbf{x}_0\right)}{q\left(\mathbf{x}_t \mid \mathbf{x}_0\right)} \\ & \propto \exp \left(-\frac{1}{2}\left(\frac{\left(\mathbf{x}_t-\sqrt{\alpha_t} \mathbf{x}_{t-1}\right)^2}{\beta_t}+\frac{\left(\mathbf{x}_{t-1}-\sqrt{\alpha_{t-1}} \mathbf{x}_0\right)^2}{1-\bar{\alpha}_{t-1}}-\frac{\left(\mathbf{x}_t-\sqrt{\bar{\alpha}_t} \mathbf{x}_0\right)^2}{1-\bar{\alpha}_t}\right)\right) \\ & =\exp \left(-\frac{1}{2}\left(\left(\frac{\alpha_t}{\beta_t}+\frac{1}{1-\bar{\alpha}_{t-1}}\right) \mathbf{x}_{t-1}^2-\left(\frac{2 \sqrt{\alpha_t}}{\beta_t} \mathbf{x}_t+\frac{2 \sqrt{\bar{\alpha}_t}}{1-\bar{\alpha}_t} \mathbf{x}_0\right) \mathbf{x}_{t-1}+C\left(\mathbf{x}_t, \mathbf{x}_0\right)\right)\right)\end{aligned}pθ​(xt−1​∣xt​)=q(xt−1​∣xt​,x0​)​=q(xt​∣xt−1​,x0​)q(xt​∣x0​)q(xt−1​∣x0​)​∝exp(−21​(βt​(xt​−αt​​xt−1​)2​+1−αˉt−1​(xt−1​−αt−1​​x0​)2​−1−αˉt​(xt​−αˉt​​x0​)2​))=exp(−21​((βt​αt​​+1−αˉt−1​1​)xt−12​−(βt​2αt​​​xt​+1−αˉt​2αˉt​​​x0​)xt−1​+C(xt​,x0​)))​

高斯公式：exp⁡(−(x−μ)22σ2)=exp⁡(−12(1σ2x2−2μσ2x+μ2σ2))\exp \left(-\frac{(x-\mu)^2}{2 \sigma^2}\right)=\exp \left(-\frac{1}{2}\left(\frac{1}{\sigma^2} x^2-\frac{2 \mu}{\sigma^2} x+\frac{\mu^2}{\sigma^2}\right)\right)exp(−2σ2(x−μ)2​)=exp(−21​(σ21​x2−σ22μ​x+σ2μ2​))



与高斯公式对照可以得出 xt−1\mathbf{x}_{t-1}xt−1​ 的方差和均值如下

σ~t−1=1/(αtβt+11−αˉt−1)=1−αˉt−11−αˉt⋅βt;(在代码中去掉了βt的系数)μ~t−1(xt,x0)=(αtβtxt+αˉt1−αˉtx0)/(αtβt+11−αˉt−1)=αt(1−αˉt−1)1−αˉtxt+αˉt−1βt1−αˉtx0\begin{aligned} \tilde{\sigma}_{t-1} & =1 /\left(\frac{\alpha_t}{\beta_t}+\frac{1}{1-\bar{\alpha}_{t-1}}\right)=\frac{1-\bar{\alpha}_{t-1}}{1-\bar{\alpha}_t} \cdot \beta_t ;(在代码中去掉了\beta_t的系数)\\ \tilde{\boldsymbol{\mu}}_{t-1}\left(\mathbf{x}_t, \mathbf{x}_0\right) & =\left(\frac{\sqrt{\alpha_t}}{\beta_t} \mathbf{x}_t+\frac{\sqrt{\bar{\alpha}_t}}{1-\bar{\alpha}_t} \mathbf{x}_0\right) /\left(\frac{\alpha_t}{\beta_t}+\frac{1}{1-\bar{\alpha}_{t-1}}\right)=\frac{\sqrt{\alpha_t}\left(1-\bar{\alpha}_{t-1}\right)}{1-\bar{\alpha}_t} \mathbf{x}_t+\frac{\sqrt{\bar{\alpha}_{t-1}} \beta_t}{1-\bar{\alpha}_t} \mathbf{x}_0 \end{aligned} σ~t−1​μ~​t−1​(xt​,x0​)​=1/(βt​αt​​+1−αˉt−1​1​)=1−αˉt​1−αˉt−1​​⋅βt​;(在代码中去掉了βt​的系数)=(βt​αt​​​xt​+1−αˉt​αˉt​​​x0​)/(βt​αt​​+1−αˉt−1​1​)=1−αˉt​αt​​(1−αˉt−1​)​xt​+1−αˉt​αˉt−1​​βt​​x0​​


随后将 xt\mathbf{x}_txt​ 与 x0\mathbf{x}_0x0​ 的关系带入上式，即可得到xt−1\mathbf{x}_{t-1}xt−1​均值最终结果:

μ~t−1=αt(1−αˉt−1)1−αˉtxt+αˉt−1βt1−αˉt1αˉt(xt−1−αˉtzt)=1αt(xt−βt1−αˉtzt);未知量由x0变成了z\begin{aligned} \tilde{\boldsymbol{\mu}}_{t-1} & =\frac{\sqrt{\alpha_t}\left(1-\bar{\alpha}_{t-1}\right)}{1-\bar{\alpha}_t} \mathbf{x}_t+\frac{\sqrt{\bar{\alpha}_{t-1}} \beta_t}{1-\bar{\alpha}_t} \frac{1}{\sqrt{\bar{\alpha}_t}}\left(\mathbf{x}_t-\sqrt{1-\bar{\alpha}_t} \mathbf{z}_t\right) \\ & =\frac{1}{\sqrt{\alpha_t}}\left(\mathbf{x}_t-\frac{\beta_t}{\sqrt{1-\bar{\alpha}_t}} \mathbf{z}_t\right);未知量由\mathbf{x}_0变成了\mathbf{z} \end{aligned} μ~​t−1​​=1−αˉt​αt​​(1−αˉt−1​)​xt​+1−αˉt​αˉt−1​​βt​​αˉt​​1​(xt​−1−αˉt​​zt​)=αt​​1​(xt​−1−αˉt​​βt​​zt​);未知量由x0​变成了z​

Reverse Diffusion/Reconstruction

Unlike the forward diffusion, the reverse diffusion process requires training of a neural network model. We setup the necessary loss functions and training parameters and then perform the training.

Training

Training Loss

The original loss was proposed in Sohl-Dickstein et al. [1] as following:

K=−Eq[DKL(q(xt−1∣xt,x0)∥pθ(xt−1∣xt))+Hq(XT∣X0)−Hq(X1∣X0)−Hp(XT)]\begin{aligned} K=-\mathbb{E}_q[ & D_{K L}\left(q\left(\mathbf{x}_{t-1} \mid \mathbf{x}_t, \mathbf{x}_0\right) \| p_\theta\left(\mathbf{x}_{t-1} \mid \mathbf{x}_t\right)\right) \\ & \left.+H_q\left(\mathbf{X}_T \mid \mathbf{X}_0\right)-H_q\left(\mathbf{X}_1 \mid \mathbf{X}_0\right)-H_p\left(\mathbf{X}_T\right)\right] \end{aligned} K=−Eq​[​DKL​(q(xt−1​∣xt​,x0​)∥pθ​(xt−1​∣xt​))+Hq​(XT​∣X0​)−Hq​(X1​∣X0​)−Hp​(XT​)]​

In order to improve the results, the authors in Ho et al. [2] proposed multiple improvements. Following Parameterization of mean is proposed:

μθ(xt,t)=1αt((xt−βt1−αˉtϵθ(xt,t))\mathbf{\mu}_{\theta}(\mathbf{x}_{t}, t) = \frac{1}{\sqrt{\alpha_{t}}} \left( (\mathbf{x}_{t} - \frac{\beta_{t}}{\sqrt{1 - \bar{\alpha}}_{t}} \mathbf{\epsilon}_{\theta} (\mathbf{x}_{t}, t) \right) μθ​(xt​,t)=αt​​1​((xt​−1−αˉ​t​βt​​ϵθ​(xt​,t))

further, variance is taken as constant and the step for reverse diffusion then becomes:

xt−1=1αt(xt−1−αt1−αtˉϵθ(xt,t))+σtz\mathbf{x}_{t-1} = \frac{1}{\sqrt{\alpha_{t}}} \left( \mathbf{x}_{t} - \frac{1-\alpha_{t}}{\sqrt{1-\bar{\alpha_{t}}}} \mathbf{\epsilon}_{\theta}(\mathbf{x}_{t}, t) \right) + \sigma_{t}\mathbf{z} xt−1​=αt​​1​(xt​−1−αt​ˉ​​1−αt​​ϵθ​(xt​,t))+σt​z

After further improvements and simplifications the loss function becomes:

Lsimple=Et,x0,ϵ[∥ϵ−ϵθ(αˉtx0+1−αˉtϵ,t)∥2].\mathcal{L}_{\text{simple}}=\mathbb{E}_{t, \mathbf{x}_{0},\mathbf{\epsilon}}\left[ \Vert \epsilon - \epsilon_{\theta}(\sqrt{\bar{\alpha}_{t}}\mathbf{x}_{0} + \sqrt{1 - \bar{\alpha}_{t}}\mathbf{\epsilon}, t) \Vert^{2} \right]. Lsimple​=Et,x0​,ϵ​[∥ϵ−ϵθ​(αˉt​​x0​+1−αˉt​​ϵ,t)∥2].
